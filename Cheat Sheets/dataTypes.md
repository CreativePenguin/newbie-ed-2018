# Data Types
Data Type | Usage
--- | ---
`int` | Stores an integer
`double` | Stores a real number
`boolean` | Stores a true or false val
`String` | Stores a combination of characters

# Comparators
Symbol | Usage
--- | ---
< | less than
\> | greater than
<= | less than or equal to
\>= | greater than or equal to
== | is equal to
!= | not equal to

# Logical Operators
Symbol | Usage
--- | ---
&& | and
|| | or
! | not
